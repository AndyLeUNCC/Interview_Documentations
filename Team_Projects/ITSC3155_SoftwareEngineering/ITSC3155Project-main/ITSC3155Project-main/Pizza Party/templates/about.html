Welcome to our brand new app, Pizza Party!

We are a group of six relatively young, ethnically diverse and highly motivated software developers located in and around Charlotte, North Carolina, USA. Our motivation for creating this app is to create a convenient and efficient means for bringing people together to enjoy the thing that brings people together the most: food!
